Openbook is our answer to a long-lasting, honest, privacy-friendly and fun social network! Make the social network your own with themes and over 30 reactions, share privately with friends with Openbook Circles and discover new interests and make friends by joining or creating a community.

Crowd-founded by people from over 100 countries, Openbook is a movement to create a responsible, sustainable, fair and enjoyable social network for a more prosperous tomorrow.

Join the revolution!

Specific features:

* Share text, image and video posts
* Create audiences such as Friends, Family and Work and share content privately with them
* Create communities and make friends with people that share your interests
* Discover new interests by joining communities
* Customise the app colours
